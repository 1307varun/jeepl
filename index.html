What is signage?

Signage is the design or use of signs and symbols to communicate a
message.signage also means signs collectively or being considered as a
The term signage is documented to have been popularized in 1975 to 1980.
Signs are any kind of visual graphics created to display information to a particular
audience.This is typically manifested in the form of wayfinding information in
or on the inside and outside buildings.signs vary in form and size based on location
places such as streets and intent, from more expansive banners,billboards and murals,
